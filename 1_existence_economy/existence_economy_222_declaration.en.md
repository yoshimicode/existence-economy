# Existence Economy™ Declaration
## Official Text — 222 Declaration

**February 22, 2026**

I hereby declare:

In an age where AI increasingly participates in decision-making,
human dignity must no longer remain an abstract principle,
but be implemented as a structural foundation.

Existence Economy™ is an attempt
to redesign the operating system of the economy—
shifting its basis
from productivity and output
to the protection of human existence and dignity itself.

This is not an ideology.
It is a design.

I commit to implementing Existence Economy™
through the following four mechanisms:

1. Human Reference Point (HRP)
2. Dignity Guardrails
3. Resonance / Existence Logs
4. Interoperability

Rather than placing dignity above efficiency,
we reposition economy and technology
upon dignity itself.

This declaration is recorded
as a civilizational timestamp.

**YOSHIMI (Yoshimi Nakane)**
