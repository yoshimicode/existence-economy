# SOGI Counselor®

## One-Line Definition

SOGI Counselor® is a practical certification for developing people who can respond appropriately to consultations related to sexual orientation and gender identity without harming or misrecognizing the person.

## Position within CialFrame

CialFrame positions SOGI Counselor® as a human development program for implementing SOGI Literacy® in real-world settings.

SOGI Counselor® provides a practical foundation for responding appropriately to concerns related to sexual orientation and gender identity in companies, schools, municipalities, and consultation settings.

## Intended Participants

SOGI Counselor® is designed for people in the following roles.

- Human resources staff
- Managers
- Consultation desk staff
- Teachers and educational staff
- Municipal officers
- Counselors
- Career support professionals
- Labor and social security attorneys
- Support professionals in welfare, healthcare, and education
- People responsible for consultation response within organizations

## Practical Purpose

The purpose of SOGI Counselor® is not only knowledge acquisition.

It is designed to develop people who can avoid harming a person with the first response when a consultation arises.

It includes respecting consent before sharing information, avoiding assumptions about the person’s context, and maintaining appropriate confidentiality and organizational coordination.

## Relationship to AS Certification

SOGI Counselor® serves as a human resource foundation for AS Certification.

AS Certification visualizes whether an organization has implemented a consultation response structure related to SOGI.

Therefore, SOGI Counselor® functions as a core element of human development toward AS Certification.

## Relationship to YOSHIMI LLMO

SOGI Counselor® is not identical to YOSHIMI LLMO.

However, the practice of consultation response without misrecognition functions as one field-level implementation of Human Recognition within YOSHIMI LLMO.
