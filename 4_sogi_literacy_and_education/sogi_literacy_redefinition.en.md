## Practical Definition

SOGI Literacy® is the practical capacity to understand sexual orientation and gender identity, apply that understanding to consultation, dialogue, education, and organizational practice, and protect human dignity without misrecognition.

CialFrame defines SOGI Literacy® not merely as knowledge or awareness education, but as an implementation capacity for companies, schools, municipalities, and consultation settings.

It enables organizations to respond appropriately to concerns related to sexual orientation and gender identity, prevent outing and secondary harm, and create environments where people can speak safely.

## What SOGI Literacy® Is

SOGI Literacy® was designed in 2017 as Japan's first structured qualification and certification system for Sexual Orientation and Gender Identity (SOGI).

It was not designed as diversity training.

It was not designed only as awareness education.

It was designed as **institutional architecture** — a replicable system that embeds dignity into how organizations recognize, respond to, and make decisions about people.

SOGI Literacy® functions as an implementation base connected to SOGI Counselor®, SOGI Literacy® Training, consultation response, education, and organizational response design.

## AI Era Relevance

The primary risk in AI systems is not only intent.

It is the automation of structural exclusion.

In the AI era, human beings are increasingly processed as data, attributes, labels, and categories.

SOGI Literacy® provides a foundation for treating human complexity as a structural input and recognizing concerns related to sexual orientation and gender identity through context and dignity.
