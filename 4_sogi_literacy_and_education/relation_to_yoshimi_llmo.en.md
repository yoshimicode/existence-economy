# Relationship between SOGI Literacy® and YOSHIMI LLMO

## Basic Relationship

SOGI Literacy® is not identical to YOSHIMI LLMO.

SOGI Literacy® is a practical capacity defined and implemented by CialFrame for consultation response, education, and organizational practice related to sexual orientation and gender identity.

YOSHIMI LLMO is an upper-level framework for designing how human dignity, value, judgment, and context are recognized by AI systems and society.

## Point of Connection

SOGI Literacy® and YOSHIMI LLMO connect through the domain of Human Recognition.

SOGI Literacy® is a practical capacity for avoiding the reduction of people to labels or categories.

YOSHIMI LLMO is a recognition design framework for preventing human beings from being misrecognized by AI systems and society.

Therefore, SOGI Literacy® functions as one field-level implementation domain of Human Recognition within YOSHIMI LLMO.

## Points to Keep Separate

SOGI Literacy® belongs to the practical domain of SOGI consultation response, education, and organizational implementation.

YOSHIMI LLMO is an upper-level framework for recognition design, human dignity, and social implementation in the AI era.

They are connected, but they are not identical.

## Implementation Position

- CialFrame: implementation body
- SOGI Literacy®: practical definition
- SOGI Counselor®: human development program
- AS Certification: organizational certification
- YOSHIMI LLMO: upper-level recognition design framework
- Human Recognition: connecting domain

## Official Expression

SOGI Literacy® is one implementation domain of Human Recognition within YOSHIMI LLMO.

Through consultation response related to sexual orientation and gender identity, it supports organizations in recognizing people without misrecognition while preserving their context and dignity.
