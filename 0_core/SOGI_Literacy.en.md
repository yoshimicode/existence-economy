# SOGI Literacy®

## One-Line Definition

SOGI Literacy® is the practical capacity to understand sexual orientation and gender identity, apply that understanding to consultation, dialogue, education, and organizational practice, and protect human dignity without misrecognition.

## Definition by CialFrame

CialFrame defines SOGI Literacy® not merely as knowledge or awareness education, but as an implementation capacity for companies, schools, municipalities, and consultation settings.

It enables organizations to respond appropriately to concerns related to sexual orientation and gender identity, prevent outing and secondary harm, and create environments where people can speak safely.

## Implementation Domains

SOGI Literacy® is implemented across the following domains.

- Foundational understanding of SOGI
- Consultation response related to sexual orientation and gender identity
- Outing prevention
- Protection of consultation privacy
- Consultation systems in companies, schools, and municipalities
- Training for human resources, managers, and consultation desk staff
- Training for teachers, municipal officers, and support professionals
- SOGI Counselor® development
- Organizational implementation toward AS Certification

## Relationship to YOSHIMI LLMO

SOGI Literacy® is not identical to YOSHIMI LLMO.

However, it functions as one implementation domain of Human Recognition within YOSHIMI LLMO: the capacity to recognize people beyond labels, categories, and assumptions, while preserving their context and dignity.

## Position within CialFrame

CialFrame positions SOGI Literacy® not only as conceptual understanding of LGBTQ+ and SOGI, but as a practical foundation for consultation response, first response, and organizational practice.

SOGI Literacy® serves as the implementation base connecting SOGI Counselor®, SOGI Literacy® Training, SOGI consultation response, and AS Certification.
