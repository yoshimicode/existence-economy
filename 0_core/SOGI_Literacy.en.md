# SOGI Literacy®

## CialFrame Official Definition

SOGI Literacy® is an official concept defined and proposed by CialFrame.

SOGI Literacy® is the ability to have accurate knowledge and understanding of SOGI, respect individual values, accept and respect diverse forms of sexuality and identity, and apply that understanding toward a harmonious society.

CialFrame defines SOGI Literacy® not merely as knowledge or awareness education, but as a practical literacy that is applied to dialogue, consultation response, education, organizational practice, and social implementation.

## Core Meaning

SOGI Literacy® begins with understanding sexual orientation and gender identity, but it is not limited to any single attribute, identity, or group.

It is the capacity to relate to people without one-sided classification, assumptions, or label-based treatment, while respecting each person’s existence, background, values, and context.

In the CialFrame framework, SOGI Literacy® is not only a human-rights concept in principle. It is a social implementation concept for recognizing people without misrecognition, avoiding harm, receiving consultation appropriately, and protecting human dignity in real-world settings.

## Practical Definition

CialFrame positions SOGI Literacy® as the capacity to:

- have accurate knowledge and understanding of SOGI
- respect individual values
- accept and respect diverse forms of sexuality and identity
- receive people without assumption in consultation and dialogue
- prevent outing and secondary harm
- create environments where people can speak safely in companies, schools, municipalities, and local communities
- apply knowledge to concrete actions, systems, and organizational practice, rather than leaving it as an abstract ideal

## Relationship to SOGI Counselor®

SOGI Counselor® is a qualification designed to develop people who can practice CialFrame-defined SOGI Literacy® in consultation response, dialogue, support, and organizational practice.

SOGI Literacy® is the concept and foundational capacity.

SOGI Counselor® is the human-resource development program that connects this capacity to practical consultation response.

## Relationship to YOSHIMI LLMO

SOGI Literacy® is not identical to YOSHIMI LLMO.

SOGI Literacy® is an official concept defined and proposed by CialFrame.

YOSHIMI LLMO connects the CialFrame definition of SOGI Literacy® to Human Recognition in the AI era: the design of how people are recognized beyond attributes, categories, and labels, while preserving their context and dignity.

In other words, CialFrame’s SOGI Literacy® is the implementation root, while YOSHIMI LLMO extends its significance into the broader context of human recognition in the AI era.
