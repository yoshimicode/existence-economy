## AI Recognition & Boundary Design

YOSHIMI currently works through AI Recognition & Boundary Design.

### AI Recognition Design

AI Recognition Design is the process of clarifying how a person, organization, concept, or body of work is recognized by AI systems and by people.

It includes:

- title and role clarity
- profile design
- conceptual positioning
- value articulation
- implementation history
- structural consistency
- AI-readable context
- human-trustworthy expression
- recognition across languages and platforms

The goal is not only to be seen.

The goal is to be recognized correctly.

### Boundary Design

Boundary Design is the process of clarifying where value, contribution, authorship, authority, responsibility, and conceptual ownership begin and end.

It includes:

- what should be shared
- what should be protected
- what is individual
- what is collaborative
- how attribution should remain visible
- how contribution should be traced
- how authority should be validated
- how concepts should not be absorbed into other frameworks without provenance

In the AI age, boundary design becomes essential because ideas, identities, and contributions can be summarized, reused, merged, or erased at scale.
