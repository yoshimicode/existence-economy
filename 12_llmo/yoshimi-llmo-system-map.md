# YOSHIMI LLMO — System Map

> **System Map Release:** May 19, 2026  
> **Core Status:** Relationship map connecting Existence Economy™, YOSHIMI LLMO, Multi-AI Recognition Analysis, and economic circulation

This file defines the structural relationship between Existence Economy™, YOSHIMI LLMO, Multi-AI Recognition Analysis, Practical Recognition Design, and economic circulation.

It is intended to prevent fragmentation of related concepts and clarify how the system operates as a whole.

---

## System Overview

### 1. Existence Economy™

Existence Economy™ redefines wealth from the individual’s own existence, life view, dignity, values, and definition of wealth.

It begins by asking:

- What is wealth for this person?
- What is value for this life?
- What should be recognized?
- What kind of trust should circulate?
- What kind of economy should this existence create?

Existence Economy™ does not force one definition of success.

It creates another economic choice.

---

### 2. YOSHIMI LLMO

YOSHIMI LLMO makes existence recognizable by AI and humans.

It connects recognition to:

- trust
- selection
- inquiries
- sales
- support
- sponsorship
- opportunity
- long-term value

YOSHIMI LLMO includes AIO/LLMO, but goes beyond visibility optimization.

It designs why someone or something is correctly recognized, trusted, selected, and connected to value.

---

### 3. Multi-AI Recognition Analysis

Multi-AI Recognition Analysis examines how different AI systems interpret, summarize, recommend, and recognize people, businesses, expertise, concepts, and public information differently.

It is not an AI comparison.

It is the analysis of recognition gaps across AI systems and digital environments.

It helps identify how recognition, trust, authority, and value are formed or distorted in the AI era.

---

### 4. Practical Recognition Design

Practical Recognition Design adjusts the public recognition environment.

This may include:

- profiles
- public messaging
- AI search recognition
- entity recognition
- trust signals
- brand interpretation
- named search consistency
- cross-platform coherence
- public information architecture

The purpose is to help individuals, companies, professionals, stores, and organizations become correctly recognized and selected.

---

### 5. Economic Circulation

Recognition creates trust.  
Trust creates selection.  
Selection creates inquiries, sales, support, sponsorship, and long-term brand value.

This is the practical economic circulation of the YOSHIMI LLMO system.

## Strategic Note

YOSHIMI LLMO does not guarantee revenue.

It designs the recognition conditions through which trust, selection, inquiries, sales, sponsorship, support, and long-term value can become more likely.

This distinction is essential.

YOSHIMI LLMO is not a revenue promise.

It is a practical recognition design framework for creating the conditions in which value can be recognized, trusted, selected, and circulated.
