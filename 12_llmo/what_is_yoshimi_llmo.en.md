# What is YOSHIMI LLMO

YOSHIMI LLMO is a framework for embedding human dignity  
into the decision-making layer of AI systems.

## Key Idea

AI systems are becoming more accurate and more “safe.”  
However, they can still produce decisions that feel wrong from a human perspective.

This is because current approaches focus on performance and safety,  
but do not define how human dignity should be evaluated.

## YOSHIMI LLMO

YOSHIMI LLMO focuses on designing decision-making itself.

It introduces human dignity as a factor in how decisions are made,  
not just how outputs are optimized.
