# Existence Economy™ — Choice Layer

> **Definition Release:** May 19, 2026  
> **Core Status:** Foundational choice layer of the Existence Economy™

Existence Economy™ begins with the redefinition of wealth.

Before value becomes money, recognition, influence, social impact, sponsorship, or support, each individual must define what “wealth” means in their own life.

For some people, wealth means money.

For others, it means relationships, freedom, dignity, peace, contribution, recognition, safety, influence, creativity, or the ability to live as oneself.

Existence Economy™ does not force one definition of success.

It creates another economic choice: a structure in which value begins from the individual’s own existence, life view, dignity, values, and definition of wealth.

---

## Core Definition

Existence Economy™ is an economic structure in which individuals redefine what wealth, happiness, dignity, and value mean in their own lives, and circulate the value arising from their existence into trust, relationships, money, support, sponsorship, opportunity, and social impact.

It does not begin from external evaluation.

It begins from the individual as the reference point of value.

---

## Why the Choice Layer Matters

Traditional economic systems often assume that value is externally defined through:

- productivity
- market price
- organizational status
- numerical performance
- social comparison
- external evaluation

Existence Economy™ adds another choice.

It allows individuals, executives, experts, companies, institutions, and organizations to define value from existence, dignity, relationships, trust, and life view.

This does not reject money.

It repositions money as one possible circulation of value, not the only definition of wealth.

## Practical Meaning

Existence Economy™ is not a spiritual concept detached from reality.

It is a structural framework for asking:

- What is wealth for this person?
- What is value for this life?
- What should be recognized?
- What kind of trust should circulate?
- What kind of economy should this existence create?
