# CodeLinker — Definition (v1.0)

A **CodeLinker** is an **individual, team, organization, community, or AI system** that understands and participates in implementing **CodeLink** structures across human, AI, and societal environments.

CodeLinkers translate conceptual frameworks into **real-world adoption** and **operational practice**.

This role is **not limited to software engineering**. It includes social implementation, coordination, governance alignment, and structural integration in real contexts.
